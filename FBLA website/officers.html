FBLA Chapter Officers

Advisor
Paula Hughes

Mrs. Paula Hughes has served as Lead Advisor for our Scotts Hill High School FBLA for 8 years. In those eight years she has provided exceptional leadership to change the dynamic of our local chapter. Through rigorous recruiting campaigns, our chapter has grown from less than 10 members to more than 50. In addition, under her initiative our chapter has successfully competed in the Region, State & National Future Business Leaders of America Leadership Conferences.

Co-Advisor
Michael Flesher

Mr. Michael Flesher has servered as a secondary advisor for two years. Under his leadership he implemented a Charitable Business Battle which has helped teach students how to manage their own mock businesses for a good cause. Michael Flesher has specialized in his business leadership skills and servicing his community throughout his entire life. He is proud to be a leader of our organization and strives to help students develop throughout their high school career.

President
Jacob Feehan

Jacob Feehan is a sophomore and has been a member of FBLA for two years. He is an enthusiastic leader and does not hesitate to help out wherever he is needed. He enjoys meeting new people and showing them how to truly serve their communities while having fun.

Vice President
Feliciti Myracle

Feliciti Myracle is a second year member of our chapter and is a sophomore at Scotts Hill. She is a bright leader of our chapter. She hopes to help individuals in our chapter reach their goals. She testifies that FBLA has been one of the greatest highlights of her high school career and encourages other to become a part of our program.

Treasurer
