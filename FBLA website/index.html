Future Business
Leaders of America

Description

Scotts Hill Future Business Leaders of America is a student business organization that aims at helping members prepare for careers in business and related fields. Through our chapter, students are encouraged to work together to achieve common goals and compete on state and national levels. We also enjoy serving our community together to help better not only the community but also to help better ourselves.
