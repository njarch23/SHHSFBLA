We Are Competitors

Our Scotts Hill FBLA chapter has been a constant force in the last few years in FBLA competitions. We have excelled at finding bright competitors that enjoy competition. These competitors have worked to be recognized on regional, state, and national levels at leadership conventions. About SLC and NLC

SLC (State Leadership Conference) and NLC (National Leadership Conference) are events hosted by the national FBLA organization that inspire students from all across the nation to compete in events that involve information related to business or techonology fields. These events help students get out and experience a competitive environment while having fun. Students must win at the previous level to advance to the next level of competition if they wish to go further in their event.
